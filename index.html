About Me

Born and raised in the vibrant city of Port-au-Prince, Haiti, I've developed a flexible approach to problem-solving, enriched by diverse perspectives. My journey began with a background in bartending and business technology, where I honed skills in communication, teamwork, and adaptability. Today, I bring these qualities to the world of software engineering. I'm committed to staying up-to-date with the latest technologies and trends, delivering high-quality solutions, and collaborating effectively in cross-functional teams.
